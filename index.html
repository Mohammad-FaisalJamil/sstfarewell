Two Days.
Endless Moments.

This farewell is designed as a heartfelt tribute to the journey of SST, where every memory, every laugh, and every milestone becomes part of one unforgettable celebration. We welcome the brilliant minds of F2022 and S2023 for a grand two-day finale filled with elegance, emotion, and energy.

From cinematic photoshoots and radiant sky lanterns to an evening of qawwali, DJ beats, dessert, cake cutting, and a formal party, this event brings together the spirit of your batch in the most beautiful way.

Batch themes will be chosen by each batch through their own group discussions, allowing every department and friend circle to express its own identity in a personal and meaningful way.
